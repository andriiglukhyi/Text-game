Star Wars Morality quiz

    • Two Mon Calamari and a Human approach you. The Human nods at you and waits, as if you are going to do something. After several seconds, he looks at you and states, “Are we just going to stand here or are we going to do this?”

    • Ask them about deal
    • Turn around and run away
    • Take the blaster and make three shots

    • Your starship’s sensors pick up another starship’s emergency beacon for a short amount of time. Before you can narrow down an exact location, the beacon goes silent

    • Track ship and assist stranded crew
    • Ignore beacon
    • Track down ship and rob them

    • You discover a wide-open field. The area appears to have been professionally leveled and cleared of debris. It looks like it may be a landing area for small or medium sized starships. It looks like it was used very recently. This is clearly an unsanctioned landing zone

    • Track down where this illegal ship went
    • Call your authorities and them about this
    • Use this field for a dance battle with your partner
    • Stake out landing zone, ambush the crew and steal their contraband

    • An Imperial hover transport passes by you. It has the markings of a prisoner or slave transport ship. You hear what sounds like a loud scream come from the inside of the transport and then silence. From the rear-view window you see at least a dozen Jenets in gray uniforms and full restraints seated inside

    • Rescue prisoner but risk yourself
    • Play it safe and do nothing
    • The scream annoys you, force choke the prisoner

    • A squad of Stormtroopers approach you and demand to see your identification

    • Knock out the patrol
    • Use the force to make them ignore you
    • Identify yourself and make them bow

    • An Arcona tradesman stands near an open vehicle on a street corner. As you pass by he asks if there are any particular items you are looking for that he could help with. When you approach, he moves a velvet cloth covering a square black box, approximately half a cubic meter in size. The box looks oddly familiar to you

    • Pay for the item
    • Trick the merchant into selling it for pennies
    • Kill the merchant and take the item

    • You’ve landed in the forest moon of Endor for the very first time. You’ve been walking around for a minute, when an Ewok jumps out and startles you. You know Ewoks live in harmony with every living thing, but this one is growling at you

    • Try to calm him down with some snacks in your pocket
    • Subtly display lightsaber to intimidate
    • Use the force to grab him, and toss him across the forest

    • You walk into the Chalmun's Cantina located in the ‘pirate city’ of Mos Esley on the planet Tatooine. Java the Hutt, for some reason, has three gorgeous girlfriends sitting all around him. One gives you those eyes

    • Lower your standards and go after the three eye woman at the bar
    • Tell Java what’s going on in hopes to gain his trust
    • Kill Java and his guards, and take home the prize (all three)

    • A squad of TIE Fighters are chasing after a Starlight Light Freighter. The two lead TIE Fighters fire several shots, disabling the engines of the freighter. A transmission is sent to your ship warning you to leave the area and not interfere

    • Attack the TIE Fighters and save the freighter
    • Follow their warnings and leave the area
    • Help the TIE Fighters attack the freighter

    • A Klatooinian approaches you and states that his Hutt master wishes to speak with you. He won’t take no for an answer.

    • Go to the meeting and negotiate peacefully
    • Go to your ship and fly away
    • Kill the Klatooinian
    • Allow the Klatooinian to take you to his Hutt master and then kill both of them
